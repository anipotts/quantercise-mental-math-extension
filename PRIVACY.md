# Privacy Policy

**Quantercise Mental Math Chrome Extension**

Last updated: January 2025

## Overview

The Quantercise Mental Math Chrome Extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

This extension:
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track your browsing activity
- Does not make any network requests
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not share data with third parties

## Local Storage

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Theme preference**: Your choice of light, dark, or system theme
- **Sound preference**: Whether sound effects are enabled
- **Best score**: Your highest score achieved
- **Last score**: Details of your most recent drill session
- **Total drills**: Count of completed drill sessions
- **Session history**: Your last 20 drill sessions (scores and statistics)

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to any external servers
- Is not shared with Quantercise or any third party
- Can be cleared by removing the extension or clearing Chrome extension data

## Permissions

The extension requests only one permission:

- **`storage`**: Required to save your preferences and scores locally

We do not request:
- Access to your browsing history
- Access to your tabs or websites
- Network access
- Any other permissions

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please visit [quantercise.com](https://quantercise.com) or open an issue on the project repository.

## Summary

**Your data stays on your device. We don't collect anything. Period.**
